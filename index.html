Process

  1. Consultation

    Our journey starts with a friendly conversation where we'll explore your ideas and define the goals for your project. This is where we get on the same page about what you envision.

  2. Proposal & Contract

    Next, I'll present a detailed project proposal, complete with a visual mock-up of your new site or app. Together, we'll finalize the plan of action to bring your vision to life.

  3. Creation

    As I design and build your project, you'll receive regular updates, so you're always in the loop. Your feedback is key as we move from concept to reality.

  4. Launch & Support

    When your project is ready to go live, I'll ensure everything runs smoothly. Plus, you'll have peace of mind knowing that ongoing support is available whenever you need it.
